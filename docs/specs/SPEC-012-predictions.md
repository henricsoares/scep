# SPEC-012 — Predictions

**Status:** Planned

This file is a placeholder for a future specification.
External AI workflows shall consume exported datasets and public APIs and shall not access
PostgreSQL directly. A future internal Predictions capability may consume approved read-only
Analytics or prediction-result contracts without granting the external AI Research Environment
access to operational persistence. It shall not redefine Analytics metrics, and prediction behavior
remains the responsibility of SPEC-012.
